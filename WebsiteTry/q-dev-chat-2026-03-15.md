## How Razorpay Works (Simple Explanation)

```
Customer clicks "Pay" → Razorpay popup opens → Customer pays (Card/UPI/NetBanking)
→ Razorpay processes payment → Sends confirmation back to your website
→ Your server sends email to customer + business owner → Order complete!
```

Your website never handles card/bank details directly — Razorpay handles all the sensitive payment stuff securely.
